# 🚧 HSE Digital Safety Toolkit

**Open-source safety templates and tools for construction industry professionals**  
Aligned with NEBOSH IGC, OSHA 29 CFR 1926, and ISO 45001:2018 standards.

---

## What Is This?

The HSE Digital Safety Toolkit is a free, open-source collection of practical safety documents, templates, and AI-assisted tools built specifically for construction site safety officers and HSE professionals.

The goal is simple: give safety officers ready-to-use digital tools that work on real construction sites — without paying for expensive software.

---

## Who Is This For?

- Safety Officers working in construction and industrial environments
- HSE Managers who need ready-made compliant templates
- Students preparing for NEBOSH, IOSH, or OSHA certifications
- Anyone who wants to build or improve a safety management system

## 📋 Available Templates

### 1. Job Safety Analysis (JSA)
**File:** `templates/JSA-Template.md`

A complete JSA form covering:
- Job information and approval sign-off
- PPE requirements checklist
- Tools and equipment inspection
- Step-by-step hazard identification with risk rating
- Emergency procedures
- Worker sign-off section
- Revision log

---

### 2. Risk Assessment
**File:** `templates/Risk-Assessment-Template.md`

A full risk assessment form covering:
- Scope and persons at risk
- Risk rating matrix (Likelihood x Severity)
- Hazard identification with initial and residual risk scores
- Hierarchy of controls
- Emergency arrangements
- CAPA tracking
- Review and revision log

---

### 3. Incident Report
**File:** `templates/Incident-Report-Template.md`

A comprehensive incident report covering:
- Incident type classification
- Injured person details
- Step-by-step incident description
- Root cause analysis — human, system, and environmental factors
- Corrective and Preventive Actions (CAPA)
- Multi-level sign-off
- Follow-up review

---

### 4. Toolbox Talk
**File:** `templates/Toolbox-Talk-Template.md`

A ready-to-use toolbox talk form covering:
- Key safety points for the topic
- Hazards and prevention measures
- Dos and Don'ts
- Lesson from incident or near miss
- Worker attendance and sign-off
- List of 12 suggested TBT topics

## ⚙️ How to Use These Templates

No software needed. All templates are in Markdown format — easy to read, copy, and edit.

**Option 1 — Use directly on GitHub:**
- Open any template file
- Click "Raw" button
- Copy the content

**Option 2 — Download:**
- Click the file
- Click the download icon (top right)

**Option 3 — Clone the repo:**
```bash
git clone https://github.com/UmairPashaa/HSE-Digital-Toolkit.git
```
