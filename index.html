About This Platform

HSE Digital Toolkit is being developed as a professional digital support system for safety work. The purpose is not just to show pages, but to build a complete toolkit where HSE professionals can assess risks, check PPE, report incidents, record inspections, access emergency details, generate PDF reports, save data, and use everything smoothly on desktop and mobile devices.
