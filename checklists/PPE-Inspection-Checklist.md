**Document Control:**
- PPE inspections must be carried out before each use for critical items (harness, respirator)
- General PPE inspection should be conducted at minimum weekly on site
- Any PPE involved in an incident or impact must be withdrawn from service immediately
- Retain completed inspection records for a minimum of 3 years
- Reference Standards: NEBOSH IGC, OSHA 29 CFR 1926.95, ISO 45001:2018
