## Section 5: Hazard Identification & Risk Control

> **Instructions:** Break the job into steps. For each step, identify what can go wrong, assess the risk, and write the control measures.

**Risk Rating Guide:**

| Likelihood | Severity | Risk Level |
|---|---|---|
| Low + Low | = | **Low** — Proceed with standard precautions |
| Low + High OR High + Low | = | **Medium** — Additional controls required |
| High + High | = | **High** — Stop work. Do not proceed without approval |
