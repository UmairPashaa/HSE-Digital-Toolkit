**Document Control:**
- Site inspections should be conducted daily for high-risk activities and at minimum weekly for all sites
- Completed checklists must be retained for a minimum of 3 years
- Unsatisfactory items must be actioned immediately — work must stop if safety is compromised
- Reference Standards: NEBOSH IGC, OSHA 29 CFR 1926, ISO 45001:2018
