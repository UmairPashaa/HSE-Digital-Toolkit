# HSE Digital Safety Framework & Toolkit

## Overview
This is an open-source initiative dedicated to digitizing **Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE)** protocols specifically for the **Construction Industry**. The project aims to integrate AI-driven reasoning into safety management systems to reduce workplace fatalities and improve global safety standards.

## Project Goals
This repository serves as a foundational toolkit for HSE Officers and professionals to:
* **Digitize NEBOSH Standards:** Translating international safety certifications into actionable digital checklists.
* **AI Risk Assessment:** Using LLMs (like Claude) to analyze site hazards and generate Job Safety Analysis (JSA) reports.
* **OSHA Compliance:** Automating the cross-referencing of site activities with regulatory requirements.
* **Multilingual Training:** Generating Tool-Box Talks (TBT) and safety materials for diverse workforces.

## Why AI?
Construction environments are dynamic and high-risk. By leveraging Claude's advanced reasoning, we can process complex technical safety data and identify root causes of potential incidents more accurately than manual methods.

## Roadmap
- [ ] Uploading NEBOSH-aligned safety templates.
- [ ] Integration of AI prompts for automated risk assessments.
- [ ] Creating a community-driven database for construction incident preventions.
