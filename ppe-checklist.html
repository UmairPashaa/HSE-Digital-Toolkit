ISO Standard PPE Checklist

Head Protection
1. Helmet shell is free from cracks, deep scratches, and damage.
2. Internal harness & chin strap are intact and fit correctly.
Eye & Face Protection
3. Safety glasses/visors are clear, untinted (for indoor), and scratch-free.
Respiratory Protection
4. Mask/respirator is appropriate for the hazard and fits tightly.
Hand Protection
5. Gloves are free from holes, tears, or chemical saturation.
Body & Visibility
6. High-visibility vest or coveralls are clean and reflective strips work.
Foot Protection
7. Safety boots have intact steel toes and anti-slip soles are not worn out.
Fall Protection
8. Harness webbing, D-rings, and lanyards are inspected and tagged valid.
