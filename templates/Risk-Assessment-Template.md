## Section 5: Hazard Identification & Risk Control

> **Instructions:**
> 1. List each hazard found in the work area or activity
> 2. Rate the risk BEFORE controls (initial risk)
> 3. Write control measures using the Hierarchy of Controls
> 4. Rate the risk AFTER controls (residual risk)

**Hierarchy of Controls (use in this order):**
1. **Elimination** — Remove the hazard completely
2. **Substitution** — Replace with something less dangerous
3. **Engineering Controls** — Isolate people from the hazard
4. **Administrative Controls** — Change the way people work
5. **PPE** — Protect the worker as last resort
